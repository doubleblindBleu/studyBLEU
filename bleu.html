To study the suitability of BLEU for code migration, we performed two experiments: (1) BLEU's correlation with semantic accuracy of translated source code with regard to the reference one when they are translated by a model, and (2) BLEU's effectiveness in comparing different SMT-based migration systems

1. BLEU's correlation with semantic score
